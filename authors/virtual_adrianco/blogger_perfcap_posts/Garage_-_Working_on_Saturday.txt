Title: Garage - Working on Saturday
URL: https://perfcap.blogspot.com/2010/03/garage-working-on-saturday.html

The concrete team were back today, putting in more rebar and tidying everything up. Chris Ramoutar the Electrician was also here putting in a 2-inch pipe to carry power to the garage. I'm having a high spec 220V and 200Amps dedicated circuit configured so that it's ready to recharge electric cars in the future.
