Title: Picture of myPhone
URL: https://perfcap.blogspot.com/2007/03/picture-of-myphone.html

Here are the CAD pictures of the phone, I made the rear case cover translucent so that some of the parts inside can be seen. The big dark block is a Treo650 battery, the block in front of it is the Telit 862 GSM/GPS module that does all the phone stuff...
