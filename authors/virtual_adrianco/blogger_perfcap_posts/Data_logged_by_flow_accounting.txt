Title: Data logged by flow accounting
URL: https://perfcap.blogspot.com/2005/03/data-logged-by-flow-accounting.html

The data comes in two forms, outgoing traffic is tagged with the userid and project of the initiating process, but incoming traffic is missing this information. Since TCP flows are captured in pairs they need to be matched up.
